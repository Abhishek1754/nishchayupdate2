Refund & Cancellation Policy

Effective Date: June 2026

1. Order Cancellation

Customers may request cancellation of an order before it has been dispatched or shipped.

Once an order has been dispatched, shipped, or handed over to a delivery partner, cancellation requests may not be accepted.

2. Non-Returnable Products

Due to hygiene, quality, safety, and regulatory reasons, certain products may not be eligible for return after delivery.

  • Edible Oils
  • Grocery Products
  • Food Products
  • FMCG Products
  • Personal Care Products
  • Consumable Products
  • Opened or Used Products
  • Perishable Products

3. Eligible Refund or Replacement Cases

  • Wrong product is delivered
  • Damaged product is delivered
  • Product is missing from shipment
  • Product has manufacturing defects
  • Product differs materially from the product ordered

4. Reporting Timeline

Claims must be reported within 48 hours of delivery.

The Company may request photographs, videos, packaging details, invoice copies, or additional evidence to verify claims.

5. Refund Method

Approved refunds shall be processed through the original payment method used during purchase.

For Cash on Delivery orders, refunds may be processed through bank transfer, UPI transfer, or other approved methods.

6. Refund Processing Timeline

Approved refunds are generally processed within 7–10 business days.

Actual credit timelines may vary depending upon banking systems, payment gateways, card issuers, UPI providers, and financial institutions.

7. Failed Transactions

If a payment is deducted but the order is not successfully placed, customers should immediately contact our support team.

After verification, eligible refunds shall be initiated as per applicable banking and payment gateway procedures.

8. Fraud Prevention

The Company reserves the right to reject refund requests that appear fraudulent, misleading, abusive, repetitive, or unsupported by sufficient evidence.

9. Limitation of Liability

Refunds shall be limited to the amount paid for the affected product or service.

The Company shall not be liable for indirect, incidental, consequential, or special damages arising from product usage or delayed delivery.

10. Final Decision

The Company's decision regarding refund eligibility, replacement, cancellation, or claim approval shall be final and binding, subject to applicable consumer protection laws.
